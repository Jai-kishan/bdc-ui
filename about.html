About Us

The Bihar Development Collective

This a joint initiative of well-meaning individuals and organizations with similar passion, zeal, competencies and beliefs towards strengthening the current development ecosystem of Bihar.

To do so, they are making a conscious attempt to develop a thriving platform, a community, which enables safe peer learning space, collaborations, mentoring, cross-learning etc.

BDC aims at “Developing & Strengthening the local “Development Ecosystem” in Bihar

Mission

Empowering Bihar’s Development Ecosystem : To collaborate with passionate individuals and organizations to build a thriving platform that supports peer learning, collaboration, mentoring, and cross-learning, thereby strengthening the local development ecosystem in Bihar.

Vision

Changing the narrative of Bihar: To create a sustainable and dynamic community of social entrepreneurs who are not only driven to bring about the sustainable solutions to the social issues in their respective geographies but match with the international standards of financial compliance and governance system

Values

Inclusivity: Instilling the essence of inclusivity of genders, religions, casts, faiths, age across all of our operations and group formation.

Togetherness and Mutual Growth: Fostering an environment of togetherness and mutual growth through safe spaces for peer learning and mentorship.

Enabling One Another: Creating an environment where enabling one another becomes a culture through sharing one’s resources, expertise, and network with other members.

BDC History & Evolution

The Journey of the Bihar Development Collective

Year of Inception

2019 - 2020

A group of like-minded people leading their respective organizations felt the need of a collective space for development organizations.

The year itself made us resilient to many obstacles that came our way but we crossed and drafted the concept note will full zeal.

Boom-Boom with Covid

2020 - 2021

The COVID made us realize the power of community. We were together supporting each other and raising funds for the different needs of the state.

As the lockdown got relaxed we physically met in September 2020 celebrating life. We also developed our website this year.

Strengthening the Bond

2021 - 2022

We believed that there were some issues that required our full attention and we emerged as a collective of organizations.

Our members started hosting physical meet-ups and we moved in the direction of adding value to the jury of different member organizations.

Becoming a Registered Body

2022 - 2023

We registered ourselves as a foundation. Different programs came into action like Masterclass, Internship Drive, and Formal Quarterly Meet-ups.

Onboarded 22 Organisations in the 1st phase of the Membership drive. The physical meet-ups started to happen more formally and it became the flagship event.

APF joins in our Mission

2023 - 2024

APF joined our hands and we raised our 1st incubation fund. This helped us to streamline our programs more firmly.

BDC hired dedicated professionals to organize our flagship programs and setting-up the processes for each.
