Vision

Changing the narrative of Bihar

To create a sustainable and dynamic community of social entrepreneurs who are not only driven to bring about the sustainable solutions to the social issues in their respective geographies but match with the international standards of financial compliance and governance system.

Mission

Empowering Bihar’s Development Ecosystem

To collaborate with passionate individuals and organizations to build a thriving platform that supports peer learning, collaboration, mentoring, and cross-learning, thereby strengthening the local development ecosystem in Bihar.

Values

Togetherness and Mutual Growth

Instilling the essence of inclusivity of genders, religions, casts, faiths, age across all of our operations and group formation.
Fostering an environment of togetherness and mutual growth through safe spaces for peer learning and mentorship.

About Us

The Bihar Development Collective

This joint initiative of passionate individuals and organizations aims to strengthen Bihar's development ecosystem. By creating a thriving platform and community, BDC fosters safe peer learning, collaboration, mentoring, and cross-learning, all with the goal of developing and enhancing the local development ecosystem in Bihar.

Our Objectives

We Focused On Empowering Bihar NGOs through providing following things:

Collaboration

Creating a database of quality workers, assessing their impact, and connecting them for mutual needs. Arranging cross-learning visits and fostering networking as and when needed.
